# ZoomChat — User Guide

A short walkthrough of the things you'll use day-to-day. Most people are
running with it confidently in under 10 minutes.

---

## What ZoomChat does

ZoomChat dispatches a friendly bot into your Zoom meetings, pulls every
chat message into a single unified feed, and lets you save the
highlights, reply back into the room, or pop the conversation onto a
secondary screen for a host or presenter to read live.

## Getting started

### 1. Open the app

You have two options:

- **The ZoomChat .app** that was installed on your computer — double-click
  the icon (typically in your Applications folder)
- **Any web browser** — visit
  [zoomchat.ryteproductions.com](https://zoomchat.ryteproductions.com)

Both behave identically. The .app is just a tidy frame around the same
thing.

### 2. Sign up or sign in

signed in instantly. (Check your inbox shortly after for a one-click

## The basics

### Connect to a meeting

In the **Connect** tab (left sidebar):

1. Paste the Zoom **Meeting ID** (just digits — spaces and dashes are
   fine)
2. Paste the **Passcode** if the meeting has one
3. Give the room a **friendly name** (e.g. "Main Stage", "Q&A Room") and
   pick a color
4. Set the **Bot Display Name** — this is how the bot appears to people
   in the meeting (e.g. "Audience Q&A" or "Producer Notes")
5. Click **Connect**

Within seconds, the bot joins your Zoom meeting. Once admitted from the
waiting room (if the host has one enabled), chat messages start
streaming into the feed.

### Reading the feed

The middle of the screen shows every message from every connected room,
newest at the bottom. Each message shows the sender's name, the room
they're in (color-coded), the timestamp, and the message text.

Use the **Rooms** tab (left sidebar) to filter the feed to just one
room when you need focus.

### Saving messages

Click the **bookmark icon** on any message to save it. Saved messages
appear in the **Saved** tab (left sidebar). From there you can:

- **Copy** the message as plain text (with attribution)
- **Export as PNG** — a square, social-ready quote card (1080×1080)
- **Export all to CSV** — for record-keeping or reporting
- **Add a note** to remember why you saved it

### Replying into a room

Each message has a **Reply** button. Click it, type your message, hit
**⌘ + Return**. The reply goes into that specific Zoom meeting via your
bot.

### Broadcasting to every room

At the top of the feed, click the **Broadcast** composer to send the
same message into every connected meeting at once. Useful for "we'll
start in 5 minutes" announcements during multi-room events.

## Tools for live events

### Presenter display

Click the **pop-out icon** in the header (next to the gear). A
borderless window opens — drop it on a secondary monitor, double-click
to go full-screen. Drag anywhere to reposition.

Use this for a host, presenter, or screen behind the speaker so they
can read audience chat live. Font scale is independently adjustable in
**Settings → Typography → Display View Scale** (1× to 3×).

### Rosters

If you run the same event regularly, save your meeting list as a
**Roster** in the Rosters tab. Then before the show, click **Deploy**
once and every bot gets dispatched in parallel. Perfect for recovery if
you ever need to close and reopen the app mid-event.

### Sessions

Every time the app launches, a new session starts to group everything
that happens. You can:

- **Rename** the current session to something meaningful for the event
  ("Acme Q3 Kickoff" instead of the default timestamp)
- **End session and start new** between events so the feed and saved
  list reset cleanly
- **Browse past sessions** to see message counts and download CSVs of
  saved messages from any prior event

### Custom branding on quote cards

**Settings → Branding → "Brand mark (PNG exports)"** lets you put your
company name (e.g. "Acme Corp Q3 2026") in the bottom-right corner of
exported PNG quote cards. Leave blank for no footer.

## Account management

Click your **avatar (top right)** to open the account menu:

- See your current plan and (if on trial) minutes remaining
- **Upgrade — view plans** — pick or switch tiers (trial / canceled users)
- **Manage subscription** — opens the Stripe Customer Portal where you
  can change payment method, switch tiers, download invoices, or cancel
  (paid users)
- **Sign out**

### Team members (admin only)

If you're an admin, **Settings → Team** lets you:

- Invite a teammate by email (they get a link to set their password)
- Change roles (admin / operator)
- Remove members
- Revoke pending invitations

Operators can use everything except billing and team management. Admins
can do it all.

## Tips

- **⌘R** in the .app pulls the latest version without quitting
- **Esc** in the presenter window exits full-screen first, then closes
  the window on a second press
- **Customize colors and fonts** in Settings — six theme presets, plus
  full custom colors. Changes also apply to the presenter window via
  live sync.
- The bot can only see chat — it does **not** see or record video or
  audio. It's a chat-only observer.
